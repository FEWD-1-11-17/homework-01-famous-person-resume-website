Nathan Explosion

Trivia

  • In Dethcarraldo, Nathan's ancient animal form is revealed to be a Crocodilia, most likely an alligator.
  • In Dethgov, Nathan is made Governor of Florida after the public execution of Governor Kip Slaughter by rabid Dethklok fans. This alarms The Tribunal, causing them to go Purple Alert.
  • Nathan is the only Dethklok member to be shown wearing glasses besides Offdensen and Murderface, though they are only for reading.
  • Nathan's role as the main songwriter for Dethklok echoes Brendon Small's role in writing music for Metalocalypse.
  • In the episode Dethcarraldo, Nathan is revealed to be 1/4 Yaneemango, a savage tribe from South America.
  • His name is a rather obvious pun on the term "Napalm Explosion".
  • Nathan Explosion made a quick cameo appearance in the BATUPDATE video "WHERE IS BATMETAL?!", which is on the Russian animation studio Red MEDUSA's YouTube channel ArhyBES.
