Kyary Pamyu Pamyu is the reigning J-pop princess. Her music has become increasingly popular abroad thanks to the success of her smash hit PONPONPON.

Yasutaka Nakata

KPP's music to date has been written and produced by Yasutaka Nakata , one of Japan's most influential modern pop writes. He is also the primary song writer for other J-pop acts such as Perfume, Meg, and Capsule.
