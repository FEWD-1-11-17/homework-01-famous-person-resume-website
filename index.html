Minerva "Minnie" Mouse is best known as the girlfriend of Mickey Mouse. Minnie made her debut in the Disney animated short Steamboat Willie, released on November 18th, 1928, and has been delighting audiences ever since. Minnie can be on the Disney Junior show Mickey Mouse Clubhouse as well as in many Disney animated cartoons. Minnie's iconic polka dots have made her a style icon, and she has been seen in over 100 different outfits. Minnie is available to meet guests at all of the Disney parks.
