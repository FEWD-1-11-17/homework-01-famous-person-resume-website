My Interests

Hi, I'm Minerva "Minnie" Mouse, and you may know me as the girlfriend of the one and only Mickey Mouse. I made my debut in the Disney animated short Steamboat Willie in November 1928, and I've been delighting audiences ever since. I've been described as sweet-natured, and I always love to have fun.

My favorite hobbies include fashion, spending time with my sweetie, Mickey, and having fun with friends at the Disney Parks.
