Danny Rand

  • Alias: The Immortal Iron Fist
  • Birthplace: New York City
  • Abilities: Master martial artist, power of "Iron Fist" enhances stamina, speed, strength, reflexes, and durability

Biography

The son of a wealthy industrialist, Danny Rand was orphaned as a 10-year old while on a family quest to find the mystical city of K'un L'un -- a place with kung fu fighting and immortal monks who worship a dragon. Once a generation, the best kung fu warrior in the city fights the dragon to get magical powers of the Iron Fist. Danny won, then decided to return the New York City to kick butt.
