Celia Cruz

The Queen of Salsa

Experiences

  • Became lead singer the Sonora Matancera touring all of Latin America.
  • Joined accomplished pianist Larry Harlow and was soon headlining a concert at New York's Carnegie Hall.
  • Member of Fania All Stars
  • Performed with the likes of Larry Harlow, Dolores del Río Tito Puente and Willie Colón
  • Won a Grammy Award for Best Tropical Latin Performance.
  • Awarded the National Medal of Arts by President Bill Clinton Cruz
  • Honored by the US Postal Service with a commemorative postage stamp honoring Latin music greats

Interests

  • The Celia Cruz Foundation offering financial aid to music students of Latino descent.
  • The Celia Cruz Foundation make great contributions to the fight against cancer.
  • Passionate about a free and democratic Cuba.
