Bruce Springsteen

Albums

Springsteen has released records steadily since 1973, and is widely known for his brand of heartland rock infused with pop hooks, poetic lyrics, and Americana sentiments centered on his native New Jersey. The E Street Band have been Springsteen's primary backing band since 1972 and have appeared on a majority of his 18 studio albums and 16 live releases.

Top Studio Albums:
  • Greetings from Asbury Park, N.J. - Released: 1973
  • Born to Run - Released: 1975
  • The River - Released: 1980
  • Born in the U.S.A. - Released: 1984

Awards

Grammys:

Springsteen has won 20 Grammy Awards out of 49 Nominations.

Golden Globes:
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Original Song for "Streets of Philadelphia" in 1994
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Original Song for "The Wrestler" in 2009
Academy Awards:
  • Academy Award for Best Original Song, 1994, "Streets of Philadelphia" from Philadelphia
Other Accolades:
  • Inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame, 1999
  • Inducted into the New Jersey Hall of Fame, 2007
  • 2009 Kennedy Center Honors recipient
  • 2016 Presidential Medal of Freedom recipient
